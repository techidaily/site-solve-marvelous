## The AI Pulse Podcast: Inside Episodes 2 and 3

###### by Max Vermeir, Senior Director of AI Strategy

This technology could potentially unleash enormous productivity, as well as lift people up and remove the highly repetitive, labor-intensive, error-prone jobs, creating more meaningful opportunities for all of us to enjoy the benefits of AI technology.

Andrew Pery, AI Ethics Evangelist

Are you excited about the transformative potential of artificial intelligence for your business, your career, your life, and society at large? If so, you won't want to miss the latest episode of our AI Pulse podcast, where I engage in a thought-provoking conversation with Andrew Pery, a renowned expert in AI ethics and legislation.

In our platform, we strive to keep you informed and ahead of the curve with the most recent advancements in artificial intelligence and intelligent automation. The rapid evolution of AI technology is not just a topic of conversation; it's a driving force behind significant economic growth, projected to contribute trillions of dollars to the economy. 

While it's true that AI is transforming the job market, it's also paving the way for new opportunities and innovations that we've yet to imagine.

In this insightful two-part episode, Andrew sheds light on the importance of strategic AI regulation and the underlying reasons for it. He navigates us through the evolving AI landscape, highlighting the transformative impact on various industries and the critical role of social safety nets in supporting a dynamic workforce.

Furthermore, Andrew delves into the intricacies of emerging legislation, offering valuable insights on how businesses can adapt and thrive. He addresses key questions: How can your company's AI usage be optimized for success and safety? Are you prepared to navigate the complexities of data privacy and intellectual property?

Tune in to uncover the answers to these questions and more, and discover why adopting a voluntary code of conduct is not just beneficial but essential for any forward-thinking business leveraging AI. Let's embrace the future together, understanding the challenges and, more importantly, the vast opportunities that AI brings.
